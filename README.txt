# World Happiness Report Dashboard

## Project Overview

This is a beginner-level Power BI dashboard project built using the World Happiness Report dataset.

The dashboard analyzes happiness scores across countries and explores the impact of various socio-economic factors such as GDP, social support, healthy life expectancy, freedom to make life choices, and perceptions of corruption.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project is to visualize and analyze factors contributing to happiness levels across different countries using Power BI.

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX (Basic)
* Excel

---

## Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards

* Country with Highest GDP
* Country with Top Happiness Score
* Country with Lowest Happiness Score

### Interactive Filters

* Country Selection

### Visualizations

* GDP per Capita vs Happiness Score
* Freedom to Make Life Choices vs Score
* Top Countries by Happiness Score
* Lowest Scoring Countries
* Geographic Distribution of Happiness
* Social Support Analysis
* GDP Comparison by Country

### Key Influencers Analysis

Identifies the factors that have the greatest impact on happiness scores.

---

## Key Insights

* Countries with higher GDP per capita generally report higher happiness scores.
* Social support shows a strong positive relationship with happiness.
* Freedom to make life choices contributes significantly to overall happiness.
* Nordic countries consistently rank among the happiest countries.
* Countries with lower economic and social indicators tend to have lower happiness scores.
